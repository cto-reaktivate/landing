0. Operational Service (Baseline Ground)

At the core of every engagement, Reaktivate provides an Operational Service — the foundation that keeps your remote office running smoothly, legally, and productively.

  • Payroll and contractor management
  • Contracts and legal administration
  • Workspace readiness and equipment support
  • Operational readiness and onboarding
  • Time tracking and reporting

Focus: no headaches, no friction — just operational clarity and productivity from day one.

1. HR Service

When you already know what profiles you need — from developers, QAs, and DevOps to team leads, tech leads, or engineering managers — we source, validate, and integrate them into your team, ensuring the right technical and leadership balance for your project.

  • Regular 1-on-1s and motivation tracking
  • Engagement and retention monitoring
  • Continuous, transparent insights that enable early action before issues affect delivery performance

You stay focused on product and delivery — we make sure the people behind it stay motivated, aligned, and growing.

Advanced HR Service extends from ongoing monitoring to organizational consulting — exploring insights, identifying patterns, and designing solutions that strengthen your culture and structure.

Scope can include: organizational design and team optimization; culture alignment and values integration; implementation of performance-management systems; leadership support and retention strategy development.

Focus: understand human dynamics, validate root causes, and co-create solutions that sustain team health and performance.

2. CTO Service

When you’re not sure what you need — or want clearer delivery visibility — our CTO function steps in.

  • Monitoring delivery results and outcomes to create data-driven visibility and ensure predictable sprint performance
  • Tracking cycle time and throughput to provide actionable insights and feedback for continuous improvement
  • Reviewing workload balance and delivery efficiency based on tracked time and output trends

Advanced CTO Service shifts from monitoring to collaboration — exploring insights, diagnosing challenges, and shaping solutions with your leadership.

Scope can include: technical or process audits; architecture and infrastructure review when tech debt or scalability issues arise; delivery flow and process design optimization; role definitions, collaboration improvements, and team-communication design; implementation support for applied improvements.

Focus: understand the problem, validate the cause, and co-create solutions that keep engineering predictable and scalable.

3. Continuous Improvement Service

The Continuous Improvement Service grows out of collaboration between HR and CTO — whether at baseline or advanced level. It’s where data turns into action, and action turns into measurable growth.

This service focuses on turning everyday feedback and delivery insights into strategic improvements — helping both teams learn, adapt, and outperform KPIs together.

Depending on depth of collaboration, it can extend into the client’s business layer — working jointly to identify growth bottlenecks, improve planning accuracy, and translate operational data into business outcomes.

Not a formal consulting phase — a living partnership built around continuous learning and smarter decisions.

Focus: grow together — outperform KPIs, not just track them.
