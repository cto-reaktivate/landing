How It Works

Reaktivate operates as your remote office — powered by HR and CTO leadership layers. When you know who you need, HR ensures motivation and alignment; when you don’t, CTO ensures visibility, results, and improvement. Both work together on a solid operational base.

Operational Service

The foundation — payroll, contracts, compliance, onboarding, and reporting — everything that keeps your remote office productive and headache‑free. You don’t pay for an office; it just works. You may later add workspace budgets to upgrade setups if needed.

HR Service

Our built‑in HR layer ensures emotional health, retention, and motivation through regular 1‑on‑1s, team monitoring, and transparent insights. You stay focused on delivery — we ensure the people behind it stay motivated and aligned. Advanced HR can include organizational consulting, performance systems, and leadership support.

CTO Service

The CTO layer provides results monitoring and delivery visibility — tracking throughput, cycle time, and efficiency per hour worked. The advanced service can extend to architecture reviews, process optimization, and collaboration design — or be performed collaboratively with your own CTO.

Continuous Improvement

Combining HR and CTO data into one loop to convert insights into performance improvements. It’s how we transform daily operations into measurable growth, evolving collaboration without bureaucracy.
